Flight Incident Dataset

This page presents some of the Global airplane accidents, failures, and hijacks data from this dataset from Kaggle. This is mainly to learn and try things out using D3 and this book - https://www.newline.co/fullstack-d3

The visualisations below show:

  • The total number of incidents by origin country and destination
  • A timeline of fatilities

Number of Incidents by Country

A "flow" map where the size of the bubbles indicates the number of incidents between 2 countries. Click on a country to display the bubbles from that country. Click again to reset. Bubbles are placed in the direction of that destination and adjusted to prevent overlapping using d3-force.

Number of Fatalities

Simple timeline with brushing ability. Click and drag to zoom, double-click to reset.
